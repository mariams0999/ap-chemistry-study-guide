Study Guide: Big Idea 1

Subatomic Particles

Particles Charge Mass in amu Position in the atom
Proton Positive Charge 1 amu Inside of the Nucleus of an atom
Electron Negative Charge 0 amu In the orbitals around the Nucleus (on the energy levels/clouds)
Neutron Neutral Charge (No Charge) 1 amu Inside of the Nucleus of an atom along with the Proton

Electron Configurations

  • Each s shell has room for one orbital with two electrons.
  • Each p shell has room for three orbitals with six electrons.
  • Each d shell has room for five orbitals with ten electrons.
  • Each f shell has room for seven orbitals with fourteen electrons.

    Ionization Energy

  • Ionization energy increases as you move to the right across a period and decreases as you go down a group.

    • Electronegativity
